Plan Your Stay

VISA
All Admission Requirements

There is no Student Visa in Taiwan. Instead, Taiwan offers 2 types of Visa for foreign students:

  1. Visitor Visa.
  2. Resident Visa.

If you are going to stay less than 6 months, you should apply for the visitor visa. If your program is longer than 6 months, you will need to apply for the resident visa.
